Nexora Solutions — Performance Review Policy
Document reference: HR-POL-005 | Effective: 1 January 2024 | Owner: HR Director

1. PURPOSE
This policy describes the performance management cycle at Nexora Solutions, including the review process, ratings, calibration, and outcomes.

2. REVIEW CYCLE
2.1 Nexora Solutions operates a bi-annual performance review cycle: Mid-Year Review (July) and Annual Review (December/January).
2.2 The Annual Review is the primary review for compensation decisions and promotion assessments.
2.3 The Mid-Year Review is a development-focused checkpoint and does not directly result in compensation changes.

3. PROCESS
3.1 Self-Assessment: Employees complete a self-assessment form in the HR system two weeks before their scheduled review meeting.
3.2 Manager Assessment: Line managers complete an independent assessment prior to the review meeting.
3.3 Review Meeting: A structured one-to-one meeting between employee and manager to discuss performance, development goals, and feedback.
3.4 Calibration: Department heads and HR conduct calibration sessions to ensure consistency of ratings across teams.

4. RATINGS
Performance is assessed on a five-point scale:
- 5 — Exceptional: Significantly exceeds expectations across all areas
- 4 — Exceeds Expectations: Consistently performs above role requirements
- 3 — Meets Expectations: Consistently performs in line with role requirements
- 2 — Partially Meets Expectations: Partially meets requirements; development plan required
- 1 — Does Not Meet Expectations: Performance improvement plan (PIP) initiated

5. OUTCOMES
5.1 Salary reviews are conducted annually following the Annual Review and are informed by performance ratings, market data, and budget availability.
5.2 Promotions are assessed during the Annual Review cycle and require a minimum rating of 4 in the current year.
5.3 Employees rated 1 or 2 are placed on a structured Performance Improvement Plan with 90-day review intervals.

6. CONTACT
Performance review queries should be directed to your HR Business Partner.
