Nexora Solutions — Equal Opportunities Policy
Document reference: HR-POL-015 | Effective: 1 January 2024 | Owner: HR Director

1. COMMITMENT
Nexora Solutions is committed to creating and maintaining a workplace that is free from discrimination, harassment, and victimisation. We believe that diversity of background, perspective, and experience makes us stronger.

2. PROTECTED CHARACTERISTICS
Nexora Solutions does not discriminate on the basis of: age, disability, gender reassignment, marriage and civil partnership, pregnancy and maternity, race, religion or belief, sex, or sexual orientation.

3. RECRUITMENT
3.1 Job advertisements will be written and placed to attract the widest possible pool of qualified candidates.
3.2 Interview panels will be trained in unconscious bias and structured interviewing techniques.
3.3 Recruitment decisions will be based solely on merit, skills, and role requirements.

4. DEVELOPMENT AND PROMOTION
All employees have equal access to training, development, and promotion opportunities. Promotion decisions are based on performance and capability, assessed through the standard performance review process.

5. REPORTING
Employees who experience or witness discrimination should report it to their HR Business Partner or through the anonymous whistleblowing channel. All reports are investigated promptly and confidentially.
