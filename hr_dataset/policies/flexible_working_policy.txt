Nexora Solutions — Flexible Working Policy
Document reference: HR-POL-014 | Effective: 1 January 2024 | Owner: HR Director

1. PURPOSE
Nexora Solutions recognises that employees may benefit from flexible working arrangements. This policy sets out the right to request flexible working and the process for doing so.

2. ELIGIBILITY
All employees with 26 weeks of continuous service have the statutory right to request flexible working. Nexora Solutions extends this right to employees from their first day of employment.

3. TYPES OF FLEXIBLE WORKING
Employees may request changes to: hours of work (start/finish times, compressed hours), days of work (part-time, term-time only), or location of work (remote/hybrid as covered separately in the Remote Work Policy).

4. REQUEST PROCESS
4.1 A flexible working request must be submitted in writing to the employee's line manager and HR Business Partner.
4.2 The request must specify the proposed arrangement, the desired start date, and an assessment of how the arrangement would impact the team.
4.3 Nexora Solutions will consider the request and issue a written decision within 3 months of the submission date.

5. GROUNDS FOR REFUSAL
A request may be refused on business grounds including: burden of additional cost, detrimental impact on ability to meet customer demand, inability to reorganise work among existing staff, or planned structural changes.

6. APPEAL
Employees may appeal a refused request within 10 working days of receiving the decision. Appeals are considered by the HR Director.
