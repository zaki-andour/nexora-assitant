Nexora Solutions — Grievance Procedure
Document reference: HR-POL-006 | Effective: 1 January 2024 | Owner: HR Director

1. PURPOSE
Nexora Solutions is committed to providing a fair and respectful working environment. This procedure enables employees to raise concerns or complaints about their working conditions, treatment, or colleagues in a structured and confidential manner.

2. SCOPE
This procedure applies to all employees and contractors working on Nexora Solutions premises or on behalf of Nexora Solutions.

3. INFORMAL RESOLUTION
3.1 Employees are encouraged to resolve concerns informally in the first instance by discussing the matter directly with the individual involved or with their line manager.
3.2 If the grievance involves the line manager, the employee should approach the next level of management or their HR Business Partner directly.

4. FORMAL GRIEVANCE PROCEDURE
Step 1 — Written Submission: The employee submits a written grievance to their HR Business Partner, setting out the nature of the complaint and any relevant evidence or dates.
Step 2 — Acknowledgement: HR will acknowledge receipt within 5 working days and confirm the next steps.
Step 3 — Investigation: HR appoints an investigating manager, independent of the parties involved, to conduct a fair investigation. This may include interviews, document review, and witness statements.
Step 4 — Hearing: The employee is invited to a formal grievance hearing to present their case. Employees may be accompanied by a colleague or trade union representative.
Step 5 — Outcome: The investigating manager issues a written decision within 10 working days of the hearing.
Step 6 — Appeal: If dissatisfied with the outcome, the employee may appeal in writing to the HR Director within 10 working days of receiving the decision.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
All parties involved in a grievance procedure are required to maintain strict confidentiality. Breach of confidentiality may result in disciplinary action.

6. CONTACT
To initiate a formal grievance, contact hr@nexora.com or your HR Business Partner directly.
