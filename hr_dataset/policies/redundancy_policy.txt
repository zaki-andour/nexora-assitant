Nexora Solutions — Redundancy Policy
Document reference: HR-POL-013 | Effective: 1 January 2024 | Owner: HR Director

1. PURPOSE
This policy sets out the process Nexora Solutions follows when roles are made redundant, ensuring the process is fair, transparent, and compliant with employment law.

2. DEFINITION
Redundancy occurs when a role is no longer required due to business restructuring, cessation of a product or service, or reduction in headcount requirements. Redundancy is not a reflection of the individual employee's performance.

3. SELECTION PROCESS
3.1 Where a pool of employees is at risk of redundancy, selection criteria will be applied fairly and objectively. Criteria may include: skills and qualifications, performance history, attendance record, and length of service.
3.2 Employees will be informed of the selection criteria in advance.

4. CONSULTATION
4.1 Nexora Solutions will enter into a genuine consultation period with affected employees before any redundancy decision is finalised.
4.2 For 20 or more redundancies, a minimum 45-day collective consultation period applies.
4.3 For fewer than 20 redundancies, a minimum individual consultation period of 2 weeks applies.

5. REDUNDANCY PAY
Employees with 2 or more years of continuous service are entitled to statutory redundancy pay. Nexora Solutions provides an enhanced redundancy payment of 1.5 weeks' pay per year of service, up to a maximum of 20 years.

6. NOTICE PERIODS
Standard contractual notice periods apply during redundancy. Employees may be offered pay in lieu of notice.
