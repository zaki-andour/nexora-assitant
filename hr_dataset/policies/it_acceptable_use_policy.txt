Nexora Solutions — IT Acceptable Use Policy
Document reference: HR-POL-016 | Effective: 1 January 2024 | Owner: IT Security Manager

1. PURPOSE
This policy defines acceptable use of Nexora Solutions's IT systems, devices, and network infrastructure.

2. SCOPE
All employees, contractors, and third parties accessing Nexora Solutions systems must comply with this policy.

3. ACCEPTABLE USE
3.1 Company IT systems are provided for business use. Incidental personal use is permitted provided it does not consume excessive bandwidth, create legal risk, or interfere with work.
3.2 Employees must use strong passwords (minimum 12 characters, including upper/lowercase, numbers, and symbols) and must not share credentials with any other person.
3.3 Multi-factor authentication is mandatory for all systems where it is available.

4. PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES
The following are strictly prohibited: accessing, downloading or distributing illegal content; attempting to access systems or data without authorisation; installing unauthorised software; disabling security tools or firewalls; using company systems for cryptocurrency mining or personal commercial activities.

5. MONITORING
Nexora Solutions reserves the right to monitor use of its IT systems for security and compliance purposes. Employees have no expectation of privacy when using company systems.

6. INCIDENT REPORTING
Any security incident, suspected breach, or unusual system behaviour must be reported to the IT Security team immediately at security@nexora.com.
