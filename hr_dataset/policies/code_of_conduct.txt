Nexora Solutions — Code of Conduct
Document reference: HR-POL-010 | Effective: 1 January 2024 | Owner: CEO

1. OUR VALUES
Nexora Solutions operates on the principles of integrity, respect, innovation, and accountability. Every employee is expected to embody these values in their daily conduct.

2. PROFESSIONAL CONDUCT
2.1 Employees are expected to treat all colleagues, clients, and partners with respect and professionalism.
2.2 Harassment, bullying, or discrimination of any kind — based on gender, age, ethnicity, religion, disability, sexual orientation, or any other characteristic — will not be tolerated and may result in summary dismissal.
2.3 Employees must not engage in activities that create a conflict of interest with their duties at Nexora Solutions. Any potential conflict must be disclosed to HR in writing.

3. DATA AND CONFIDENTIALITY
3.1 Employees must handle personal data, client data, and proprietary company information with the highest standard of care.
3.2 Confidential information must not be shared externally without authorisation. Non-disclosure agreements govern all external sharing of sensitive materials.

4. USE OF COMPANY RESOURCES
4.1 Company devices, systems, and resources are provided for business purposes. Incidental personal use is permitted but must not interfere with work responsibilities.
4.2 Accessing, storing or distributing offensive, illegal or inappropriate content on company systems is grounds for disciplinary action.

5. SOCIAL MEDIA
Employees must not publish content that could damage Nexora Solutions's reputation or disclose confidential information. Personal opinions shared publicly must be clearly identified as personal, not representative of Nexora Solutions.

6. REPORTING CONCERNS
Employees who witness a breach of this code are encouraged to report it through the company's whistleblowing channel or directly to their HR Business Partner. All reports are treated confidentially.
