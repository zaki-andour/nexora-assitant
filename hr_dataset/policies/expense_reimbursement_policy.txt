Nexora Solutions — Expense Reimbursement Policy
Document reference: HR-POL-004 | Effective: 1 January 2024 | Owner: Finance Director

1. PURPOSE
This policy outlines the procedures for claiming reimbursement of legitimate business expenses incurred by employees in the course of their duties.

2. ELIGIBLE EXPENSES
The following categories of expenses are eligible for reimbursement subject to the limits set out in this policy:
- Business travel (flights, trains, taxis, car hire)
- Accommodation for overnight business trips
- Client entertainment and business meals
- Conference and training fees
- Home office equipment (subject to IT approval)
- Mobile phone and internet (where role requires remote connectivity)

3. EXPENSE LIMITS
3.1 Accommodation: Maximum £180 per night in London and major European capitals; £120 per night elsewhere.
3.2 Meals (without client): Maximum £40 per day.
3.3 Client entertainment: Maximum £75 per person per event. Requires prior approval from Head of Department for amounts exceeding £300 total.
3.4 Air travel: Economy class for flights under 5 hours. Business class permitted for flights over 5 hours subject to Finance Director approval.
3.5 Taxi / ride-hailing: Permitted for business travel. Personal commuting costs are not reimbursable.

4. SUBMISSION PROCESS
4.1 All expenses must be submitted within 30 calendar days of the date the expense was incurred.
4.2 Receipts must be provided for all individual expenses exceeding £10.
4.3 Expenses are submitted via the company expense management system and require line manager approval.
4.4 Approved expenses are reimbursed within the next monthly payroll cycle.

5. NON-ELIGIBLE EXPENSES
The following are not eligible for reimbursement: personal purchases, alcohol consumed outside of approved client entertainment, fines or penalties, and expenses incurred by family members.

6. CONTACT
Finance queries regarding expenses should be directed to finance@nexora.com.
