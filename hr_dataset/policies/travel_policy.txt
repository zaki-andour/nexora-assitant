Nexora Solutions — Business Travel Policy
Document reference: HR-POL-012 | Effective: 1 January 2024 | Owner: Finance Director

1. PURPOSE
This policy governs all business travel undertaken by employees on behalf of Nexora Solutions and ensures travel is conducted safely, efficiently, and cost-effectively.

2. BOOKING
2.1 All business travel must be booked through the company's approved travel management platform.
2.2 Flights should be booked at least 14 days in advance where possible to minimise cost.
2.3 Economy class is the standard for all flights. Business class is permitted for flights exceeding 5 hours subject to Finance Director approval.

3. ACCOMMODATION
Accommodation must be booked through the approved travel platform. The maximum nightly rate is £180 in London and major capitals, £120 elsewhere. Exceptions require Finance Director approval.

4. INTERNATIONAL TRAVEL
4.1 All international travel requires pre-approval from the employee's Head of Department at least 5 working days in advance.
4.2 Employees travelling to regions designated as high-risk by the Foreign Office or equivalent must obtain explicit approval from the CEO and must register their travel with HR.
4.3 Travel insurance is provided automatically for all approved business travel. Employees must carry their travel insurance details when travelling.

5. HEALTH AND SAFETY
Employees must follow all health and safety guidance relevant to their travel destination. Any illness or injury during a business trip must be reported to HR and the employee's manager within 24 hours.

6. CONTACT
finance@nexora.com for travel booking queries; hr@nexora.com for health and safety during travel.
