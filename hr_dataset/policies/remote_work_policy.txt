Nexora Solutions — Remote Work Policy
Document reference: HR-POL-002 | Effective: 1 March 2024 | Owner: HR Director

1. PURPOSE
Nexora Solutions supports flexible working arrangements that enable employees to balance professional effectiveness with personal wellbeing. This policy defines the conditions under which remote work is permitted.

2. SCOPE
This policy applies to all permanent employees. Contractors may work remotely subject to the terms of their individual contracts and with explicit approval from their engagement manager.

3. REMOTE WORK ENTITLEMENT
3.1 Eligible employees may work remotely for up to 3 days per week, subject to manager approval and role suitability.
3.2 A minimum of 2 days per week in the office is required for all permanent employees unless a full-remote exception has been granted in writing by the employee's Head of Department.
3.3 Roles designated as on-site (e.g. IT infrastructure, office operations) are not eligible for remote work without explicit approval.
3.4 Interns and employees within their first 3 months of employment are expected to work on-site full-time unless otherwise agreed.

4. APPROVAL PROCESS
4.1 Employees must discuss remote working arrangements with their direct line manager before commencing.
4.2 Remote work requests exceeding 3 days per week require written approval from both the line manager and the Head of Department.
4.3 Approved remote arrangements are reviewed annually or when a change in role occurs.

5. EQUIPMENT AND CONNECTIVITY
5.1 Nexora Solutions provides a laptop and necessary software for remote work. Employees are responsible for maintaining a secure and productive home working environment.
5.2 Employees must use the company VPN at all times when accessing internal systems remotely.
5.3 Requests for additional equipment (external monitor, keyboard, etc.) may be submitted to IT for approval. Approved items are subject to a budget of £300 per employee per year.

6. SECURITY REQUIREMENTS
6.1 Employees must ensure that confidential information is not visible to third parties during remote work sessions.
6.2 All work must be conducted on company-issued devices. Use of personal devices for work purposes is not permitted without explicit IT security approval.
6.3 Any security incident occurring during remote work must be reported immediately to the IT Security team.

7. PERFORMANCE AND AVAILABILITY
Remote employees are expected to maintain the same level of availability and responsiveness as they would in the office during core hours (09:00–17:00 local time).

8. CONTACT
Remote work queries should be directed to your line manager or HR Business Partner.
