Nexora Solutions — Learning and Development Policy
Document reference: HR-POL-017 | Effective: 1 January 2024 | Owner: HR Director

1. PURPOSE
Nexora Solutions invests in the continuous development of its employees. This policy outlines the learning and development support available and the process for accessing it.

2. MANDATORY TRAINING
All employees must complete the following training modules within their first 30 days and renew them annually: data protection and GDPR, information security awareness, code of conduct, anti-bribery and corruption, and health and safety fundamentals.

3. ROLE-SPECIFIC TRAINING
Line managers are responsible for identifying role-specific training needs during performance reviews and onboarding. Agreed training is recorded in the employee's development plan in the HR system.

4. EXTERNAL TRAINING AND CERTIFICATIONS
4.1 Employees may request funding for external training or professional certifications relevant to their role.
4.2 Requests must be submitted to the line manager and HR Business Partner. Approved requests are funded from the departmental L&D budget.
4.3 The annual L&D budget allocation per employee is £1,500 for Band1–Band2 and £2,500 for Band3–Band5.
4.4 Employees who leave within 12 months of completing company-funded training valued above £500 may be required to repay a pro-rated amount.

5. INTERNAL LEARNING
Nexora Solutions provides access to an internal learning platform with courses covering technical skills, management capabilities, and soft skills. All employees have unlimited access to the platform.
