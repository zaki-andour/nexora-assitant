Nexora Solutions — Onboarding Policy
Document reference: HR-POL-007 | Effective: 1 January 2024 | Owner: HR Director

1. PURPOSE
This policy defines the onboarding process for all new employees and contractors joining Nexora Solutions, ensuring a consistent and effective introduction to the company, its culture, and their role.

2. PRE-ARRIVAL
2.1 HR will send the new employee a welcome pack at least 5 working days before their start date, including: offer letter, contract, IT setup instructions, first-day schedule, and key contact information.
2.2 The line manager is responsible for preparing the new employee's workstation, system access, and a 30-60-90 day plan before the start date.

3. FIRST DAY
3.1 New employees are greeted by their HR Business Partner for a company induction session covering: company values, organisational structure, HR policies, benefits, IT security, and facilities.
3.2 IT provides a device setup session including VPN configuration, email setup, and security training.
3.3 The line manager conducts a role introduction meeting covering team structure, current priorities, tools, and initial objectives.

4. FIRST 30 DAYS
4.1 The new employee completes all mandatory online training modules within the first 30 days: data protection, code of conduct, security awareness, and anti-bribery.
4.2 One-to-one meetings with key stakeholders across the team and department are scheduled during the first two weeks.
4.3 A formal check-in meeting with the line manager occurs at the end of week 2 and week 4 to review progress and address any concerns.

5. FIRST 90 DAYS
5.1 A 90-day review meeting is held between the employee, their manager, and HR to assess progress, confirm successful completion of onboarding, and establish objectives for the remainder of the year.
5.2 Any concerns regarding performance or fit identified during the 90-day period are documented and addressed through a structured support plan before the probationary period ends.

6. PROBATIONARY PERIOD
All new employees serve a 6-month probationary period. Confirmation of employment at the end of probation requires a satisfactory 90-day review and ongoing performance in line with role expectations.

7. CONTACT
Onboarding queries should be directed to hr@nexora.com or your assigned HR Business Partner.
