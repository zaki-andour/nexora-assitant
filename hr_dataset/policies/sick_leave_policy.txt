Nexora Solutions — Sick Leave and Absence Management Policy
Document reference: HR-POL-009 | Effective: 1 January 2024 | Owner: HR Director

1. ENTITLEMENT
1.1 Full-time permanent employees are entitled to up to 10 days of fully paid sick leave per calendar year.
1.2 After 10 days, statutory sick pay applies in accordance with local legislation.
1.3 Part-time employees receive sick leave entitlement on a pro-rata basis.
1.4 Contractors are not entitled to company sick pay. Statutory provisions may apply depending on jurisdiction.
1.5 Interns receive 5 days of paid sick leave per year.

2. REPORTING ABSENCE
2.1 Employees must notify their line manager and HR by 09:00 on the first day of any unplanned absence.
2.2 Notification must be by phone or messaging; email alone is not sufficient for same-day absences.
2.3 For absences exceeding 3 consecutive days, a medical certificate from a licensed physician must be submitted to HR.

3. RETURN TO WORK
3.1 A return-to-work interview is conducted by the line manager for absences of 3 or more consecutive days.
3.2 Where a pattern of short-term absences is identified, HR may request a medical assessment and implement a structured attendance improvement plan.

4. LONG-TERM SICKNESS
Employees absent for more than 4 consecutive weeks due to illness are managed under the Long-Term Sickness Absence procedure. HR will engage with the employee and their medical advisors to support a phased return to work where possible.
