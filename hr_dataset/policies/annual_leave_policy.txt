Nexora Solutions — Annual Leave Policy
Document reference: HR-POL-001 | Effective: 1 January 2024 | Owner: HR Director

1. PURPOSE
This policy sets out the entitlement to annual leave for all employees of Nexora Solutions and the procedures for requesting, approving and recording leave.

2. SCOPE
This policy applies to all permanent full-time and part-time employees. Contractors and interns are subject to separate provisions outlined in their respective contracts.

3. ENTITLEMENT
3.1 Full-time employees are entitled to 25 working days of paid annual leave per calendar year, in addition to public holidays observed in their country of employment.
3.2 Part-time employees receive annual leave on a pro-rata basis calculated against the standard 25-day entitlement.
3.3 Contractors receive a minimum of 20 days as stulated in their contract. No additional entitlement applies.
3.4 Interns receive 15 working days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
3.5 Employees with more than 5 years of continuous service receive an additional 2 days per year, up to a maximum of 30 days total.

4. ACCRUAL
Annual leave accrues from the first day of employment at a rate of 2.083 days per month for full-time employees. Leave may not be taken in excess of accrued entitlement during the first 3 months of employment.

5. REQUESTING LEAVE
5.1 All leave requests must be submitted through the HR system at least 10 working days in advance for periods of 5 or more consecutive days.
5.2 For periods of 1 to 4 days, 3 working days' advance notice is required.
5.3 Leave requests must be approved by the employee's direct line manager. In the manager's absence, requests are escalated to the department head.
5.4 Nexora Solutions reserves the right to decline leave requests where business needs require it, particularly during peak delivery periods.

6. CARRY-OVER
6.1 Employees may carry over a maximum of 5 unused leave days into the following calendar year.
6.2 Carried-over leave must be used by 31 March of the following year. Any unused days beyond this date will be forfeited.
6.3 Exceptions may be granted in cases of extended sick leave or maternity/paternity leave where the employee was unable to use leave during the year.

7. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
Employees are entitled to all public holidays applicable in their country and city of employment. Where an employee is required to work on a public holiday, they will receive a substitute day off to be taken within 60 days.

8. CONTACT
For questions regarding this policy, contact the HR team at hr@nexora.com or your HR Business Partner.
