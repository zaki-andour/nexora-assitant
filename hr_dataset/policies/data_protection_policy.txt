Nexora Solutions — Data Protection Policy
Document reference: HR-POL-011 | Effective: 1 January 2024 | Owner: Data Protection Officer

1. PURPOSE
This policy sets out how Nexora Solutions collects, processes, stores, and protects personal data in compliance with applicable data protection legislation, including GDPR where applicable.

2. DATA PRINCIPLES
Nexora Solutions commits to processing personal data lawfully, fairly, and transparently; collecting data only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes; keeping data accurate and up to date; retaining data no longer than necessary; and protecting data against unauthorised access, loss or destruction.

3. EMPLOYEE DATA
3.1 Nexora Solutions processes employee personal data for the purposes of employment administration, payroll, performance management, and legal compliance.
3.2 Employees have the right to access their personal data held by Nexora Solutions by submitting a Subject Access Request to the Data Protection Officer.
3.3 Salary and compensation data is classified as sensitive personal data and is accessible only to HR, Finance, and senior management with a legitimate business need.

4. DATA SECURITY
4.1 All personal data must be stored on company-approved systems. Local storage of personal data on unencrypted devices is prohibited.
4.2 Employees who access personal data as part of their role must complete annual data protection training.
4.3 Any data breach, actual or suspected, must be reported to the Data Protection Officer within 4 hours of discovery.

5. RETENTION
Employee personal data is retained for 7 years following the end of employment. Recruitment data for unsuccessful candidates is retained for 12 months.
