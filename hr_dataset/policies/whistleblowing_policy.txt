Nexora Solutions — Whistleblowing Policy
Document reference: HR-POL-018 | Effective: 1 January 2024 | Owner: HR Director and General Counsel

1. PURPOSE
Nexora Solutions is committed to the highest standards of ethical conduct. This policy provides a safe and confidential channel for employees to raise genuine concerns about wrongdoing, legal breaches, or ethical violations without fear of retaliation.

2. SCOPE
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party partners who have concerns about activities at Nexora Solutions.

3. WHAT TO REPORT
Concerns that may be raised under this policy include: financial fraud or misreporting, bribery or corruption, serious health and safety violations, data protection breaches, environmental violations, and cover-up of any of the above.

4. HOW TO REPORT
4.1 Employees may report concerns to their HR Business Partner, the General Counsel, or through the anonymous whistleblowing hotline at 0800-NEXORA-W.
4.2 Reports may be made anonymously. Anonymous reports will be investigated to the extent that the information provided allows.

5. PROTECTION FROM RETALIATION
Any employee who makes a good-faith report under this policy will be protected from dismissal, demotion, harassment, or any other form of retaliation. Retaliation against a whistleblower is a serious disciplinary offence.

6. INVESTIGATION
All reports will be acknowledged within 5 working days. An independent investigation will be conducted, and the outcome communicated to the reporting employee (where not anonymous) within 30 working days.

7. CONTACT
whistleblowing@nexora.com or 0800-NEXORA-W
